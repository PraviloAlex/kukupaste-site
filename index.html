Why this is not just a clipboard manager

A regular clipboard helps you find old text. KukuPaste helps you move through a work scenario: one pack loads several steps, and Ctrl+Q pastes them one by one into Claude, Codex, Cursor, CRM, email, or support chat.

How to use it with Claude, Codex, or Cursor

A pack works like a conversation script. Load a library into the stack, then paste each step wherever you work.

1. Choose a pack

Open Library and click “To stack” on the workflow you need.

2. Paste step one

In Claude/Codex/Cursor, press Ctrl+Q and add your context.

3. Follow the chain

Each next Ctrl+Q inserts the next prompt without searching or copying.

4. Save the result

Get a review, fix, reply, script, or brief in a repeatable format.
